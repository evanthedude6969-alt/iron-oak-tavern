About Iron Oak Tavern

Nestled in the heart of downtown Helen, Iron Oak Tavern is a modern gathering place where great food, craft beer, and unforgettable nights come together.

Craft Beer & Cocktails

Our bar features rotating craft beers, premium spirits, and signature cocktails carefully crafted by experienced bartenders.

Live Entertainment

From live music and trivia nights to karaoke and special events, there's always something happening at Iron Oak Tavern.

Our Mission

To create a welcoming atmosphere where friends, families, and visitors can enjoy great drinks, delicious food, and memorable experiences.
